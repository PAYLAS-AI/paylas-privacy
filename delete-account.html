Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Paylaş account, please follow the instructions below:

Via the Mobile App:

  1. Open the Paylaş app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete My Account.
  3. Confirm your action. Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours.

Alternatively:

If you are unable to access your account or prefer manual deletion, you may send us a request:

Email: paylas.app@gmail.com

Please include your registered email address and username in the request. Your data will be deleted in compliance with GDPR and local data protection regulations.
